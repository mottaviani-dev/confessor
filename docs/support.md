# Support

**Confessor: Duel of Wits** — support and contact

## Common questions

**The first launch downloads something big.**
That is the mind itself — the AI model (~2.6 GB), downloaded once over Wi-Fi. After that
the game plays fully offline.

**It says my device doesn't have enough memory.**
Confessor runs a large on-device AI and needs a recent iPhone (iPhone 15 Pro or newer
recommended). Older devices cannot load the model.

**I keep losing.**
Good — that's the game. Flattery, threats, and clever pressure harden every mind. What
reaches them costs you something real: a concrete, honest disclosure of your own. Watch
the bond ladder under the character's presence, and mind the clock.

**No sound?**
Check the in-app SOUND/MUTED toggle in the duel's top bar, and your volume. The room is
quiet by design — a low tone, a pen scratch.
