# Privacy Policy

**Confessor: Duel of Wits** — effective 6 July 2026

## The short version

Confessor collects **no data**. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no
tracking, and no servers of ours. Your conversations with the game's characters are
processed entirely on your device and never transmitted anywhere.

## What happens on your device

The game's AI (a language model) runs locally on your iPhone. Everything you type in a
duel is processed on-device and stored only on-device (your game records and session
memory live in the app's local storage). Deleting the app deletes all of it.

## The one network operation

On first run the app downloads its AI model file (~2.6 GB) over an encrypted connection
from Hugging Face's public content delivery network. This is a plain file download: no
personal data, identifiers, or gameplay content is sent with it beyond what any HTTPS
download implies (your IP address, as with any internet request). After the download the
game is fully playable offline.

## What we do NOT do

- No collection of personal information, ever
- No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs
- No advertising or third-party trackers
- No account, sign-in, or server of ours
- No sale or sharing of data — there is nothing to sell or share

## App Store privacy label

**Data Not Collected.**

## Children

Confessor is rated 17+ and is not directed at children.

## Changes

If a future version ever changes any of the above (for example, optional cloud features),
this policy will be updated before that version ships, and the App Store privacy label
with it.
