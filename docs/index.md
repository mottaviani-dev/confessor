# CONFESSOR — Duel of Wits

**A duel of wits. One mind. One secret. Talk it loose.**

You are locked in a room with a single mind — a warden, a fence, a suspect, an oracle. It
guards something. You have fourteen exchanges to talk it loose, through conversation
alone. The AI runs entirely on your iPhone: no servers, no account, nothing you say ever
leaves your hands.
